Your landing page should be optimized for B2B conversions. Here is the section-by-section copywriting layout.

*   **Headline:** Write BRD/FRD Specifications & Draw Process Models in 10 Seconds. Powered by AI.
*   **Subheadline:** Stop wasting hours in Visio, Lucidchart, and Microsoft Word. BAHub translates client meeting notes into fully linked process diagrams, user stories, and compliance-checked requirements instantly.
*   **Call-to-Action (CTA):** `Start AI Workspace - Free 14-Day Trial`
*   **Sub-CTA Hook:** *No credit card required. Set up in 1 minute.*

### Section 2: The Pain vs. The Solution (Compare Grid)
| The Old Way (Visio + Word) | The BAHub Way (Connected Workspace) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Copying and pasting requirement IDs manually. | Requirements are database objects linked to shapes. |
| Drawing blocks, lines, and handles for hours. | Paste meeting transcript; AI builds the diagram layout. |
| Circular paths and dead-ends go undetected. | Built-in validation checks compliance automatically. |
| Stale documents that don't match drawing states. | Exports auto-embed the latest Mermaid diagrams. |

### Section 3: Value Proposition Sections
*   **Feature 1: The Infinite AI Canvas**
    *   *Copy:* Paste raw text prompts or meeting logs. Our layout engine auto-arranges BPMN 2.0 flowcharts, Sequence diagrams, and Use Cases with hierarchical coordinates.
*   **Feature 2: Dynamic Inspector & Mappings**
    *   *Copy:* Bind canvas shapes to project elements (Requirements, User Stories, Risks, Stakeholders). Updates on the canvas immediately sync through the traceability tables.
*   **Feature 3: One-Click Enterprise Exporters**
    *   *Copy:* Export models to professional text formats (Mermaid code, PlantUML, Draw.io XML, BPMN 2.0 XML) or download a compiled PDF/Word document with your diagrams already embedded.
