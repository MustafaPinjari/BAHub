# BAHub — Demo Video Script (60 seconds)

> **Recording tips:** Use Loom (free) or OBS · 1920×1080 fullscreen browser · Log in as `analyst@demo.com` before recording so the dashboard is pre-filled · Slow down during the AI generation moment — that's the wow factor · Pick a subtle, upbeat background track in Loom

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:05] — Hook
*Screen: landing page hero*

> "Business Analysts spend 40% of their time copy-pasting between Jira, Confluence, Visio, and Word. BAHub replaces all of them — in one workspace."

---

### [0:05–0:15] — Requirements Grid
*Screen: REQ grid, typing a new requirement*

> "Start by capturing stakeholder requirements — each one auto-numbered, linked to a source, and instantly traceable. No more spreadsheets."

---

### [0:15–0:25] — AI Workspace ⭐ Money shot
*Screen: AI generating a BRD / user stories*

> "Hit 'Generate' — BAHub's AI reads your requirements and writes your BRD, user stories, and acceptance criteria in seconds. You edit, not start from scratch."

---

### [0:25–0:35] — Traceability Matrix
*Screen: matrix view lighting up*

> "Every requirement, story, and UAT test case is linked. Your traceability matrix builds itself — audit-ready, always up to date."

---

### [0:35–0:45] — Export & Jira Sync
*Screen: PDF export click + Jira sync panel*

> "Export a polished PDF or Word doc for stakeholders. Push stories directly to Jira. No reformatting, no copy-paste."

---

### [0:45–0:55] — Full BA Lifecycle Flash
*Screen: quick montage — Kanban → BPMN canvas → Risk register → UAT*

> "Kanban board, BPMN diagrams, risk register, SWOT analysis, meeting minutes — the full BA lifecycle, one tab."

---

### [0:55–1:00] — CTA
*Screen: landing page sign-up*

> "BAHub. Built for Business Analysts, by a Business Analyst. Free to start — try it today."

---

## Recording Checklist

| Item | Note |
|---|---|
| Pre-load demo account | Log in as `analyst@demo.com` before hitting record |
| Thumbnail frame | Pause on the traceability matrix — most visually impressive screen |
| Music | Subtle + upbeat, royalty-free (Loom library works) |
| Resolution | 1920×1080, browser fullscreen |
| Duration | Aim for 55–65 seconds — under 90s is the Product Hunt sweet spot |
| Upload | Link from the landing page hero "View Live Demo" button |
