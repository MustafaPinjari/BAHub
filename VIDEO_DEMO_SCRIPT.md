## Production Notes

### Cinematic Style Guide

**Visual Language**
- **Color Palette**: Deep blacks, purples, clean whites (match BAHub dark theme)
- **Transitions**: Smooth fades, no jarring cuts
- **Pacing**: Fast during problem, deliberate during solution
- **Camera Work**: Subtle zooms on key moments, steady otherwise

**Audio Design**
- **Voiceover**: Professional, confident, slightly warm (think Apple product videos)
- **Music**: Starts tense/minor during problem, shifts to uplifting/major during solution
- **Sound Effects**: Minimal, only for emphasis (keyboard sounds, notification chimes)
- **Silence**: Strategic pauses for impact

**Text Overlays**
- **Style**: Clean, sans-serif, white text on dark background
- **Timing**: Appear after voiceover, not before
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds max, fade out smoothly
- **Placement**: Lower third, never cover key UI elements

### Screen Recording Guidelines

**Before Recording**
- Prepare demo project with realistic data
- Practice the exact workflow 10+ times
- Ensure all AI responses are fast and impressive
- Clean desktop, close unnecessary apps
- Test audio quality

**During Recording**
- Use smooth, deliberate mouse movements
- Don't rush complex interactions
- Let AI responses complete naturally
- Show loading states briefly (they're realistic)
- Keep cursor visible but not distracting

**Technical Specs**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 8-10 Mbps
- Audio: AAC, 128kbps

### The "Wow Moments" to Emphasize

1. **AI Requirements Generation** (0:25-0:35) - Show raw text transforming into structured requirements
2. **Stakeholder Auto-suggestion** (0:15-0:25) - AI recommending stakeholders based on project type
3. **One-click BRD** (0:55-1:05) - Professional document appearing instantly
4. **Real-time Collaboration** (1:05-1:15) - Multiple cursors, live updates
5. **Launch Dashboard** (1:15-1:25) - All metrics green, project complete

### Emotional Progression

**0:00-0:15**: Stress, overwhelm, frustration (the problem)
**0:15-0:45**: Curiosity, hope, excitement (the solution)
**0:45-1:15**: Confidence, control, satisfaction (the workflow)
**1:15-1:30**: Pride, accomplishment, inspiration (the result)
